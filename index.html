Our Celebrations

Royal Weddings

From saat pheras to vidaai — we design weddings that honour every ritual with grandeur and grace.

Packages include
  • Mandap & floral décor
  • Baraat & sangeet coordination
  • Catering & hospitality
  • Photography & videography
  • Full-day event management

Haldi Ceremony

Sunlit, joyful, and fragrant — our haldi setups burst with marigolds, earthen pots, and traditional charm.

Packages include
  • Marigold garland installations
  • Floral backdrop & props
  • Traditional dhol & music
  • Thematic haldi trays
  • Organic ubtan preparation

Birthday Bashes

Whether it's a 1st birthday or a 60th milestone — we create magical celebrations for every age.

Packages include

  • Kaleere & bridal accessories
